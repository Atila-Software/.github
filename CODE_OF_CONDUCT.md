# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Atila Software is committed to providing a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in our projects.

We expect all contributors, maintainers, and collaborators to help create a space where people can work productively, ask questions, share ideas, and improve software together.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that supports a positive environment include:

- Communicating with respect and professionalism
- Giving and receiving feedback constructively
- Being mindful of different experience levels and backgrounds
- Asking for clarification before making assumptions
- Focusing discussions on the work, the users, and the project goals
- Respecting privacy, confidentiality, and project boundaries

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention
- Trolling, insults, or repeated disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sharing confidential company, customer, or project data
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional workplace

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate action when behavior does not meet this Code of Conduct.

Actions may include:

- A private clarification or warning
- Temporary limits on participation
- Removal from a discussion, issue, pull request, or project
- Escalation to company leadership or the appropriate internal contact
